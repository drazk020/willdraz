How to Play Blackjack

Objective:

Get as close to 21 as possible without going over. Beat the dealer's hand!

Card Values:

  • Number cards: Face value
  • Face cards (J, Q, K): 10 points
  • Aces: 1 or 11 (whichever is better)

How to Play:

  1. Click chips to place your bet
  2. Click "Deal" to start the round
  3. Choose "Hit" for more cards or "Stand" to stay
  4. Dealer must hit on 16, stand on 17

Special Actions:

Double: Double bet, get one card, then stand
Split: Split matching pairs into two hands
